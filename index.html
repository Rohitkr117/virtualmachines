Our web app is designed to foster connections within a university by bringing together students, faculty, alumni, and staff. It offers personalized profiles, tailored news feeds, and secure login through university credentials. The platform enables users to join groups, participate in discussions, and access an event calendar. Features like direct messaging, group chats, video conferencing for webinars, and AI chatbot assistance ensure seamless communication. Additionally, the app provides a marketplace, real-time notifications, a job and internship portal, and tools for alumni networking, creating a vibrant academic and professional community.
